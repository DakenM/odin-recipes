Stuffed Chicken Breast

Description

A cooked chicken breast with bacon bits, roasted tomato, and cheese of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bacon Strips
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese of Choice

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken Breast
  2. Cut Up Bacon and Cook
  3. Roast Tomatos
  4. Melt Cheese on Chicken
  5. Add Tomatos to Chicken
  6. Enjoy!
