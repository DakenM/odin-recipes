Grilled Cheese

Description

Creamy delish grilled cheese, easy to make and delightful to eat.

Ingredients

  • Two Slices of Bread
  • A Slice (or more) of Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add butter to your pan
  2. Put cheese in between bread
  3. Add sandwhich to pan
  4. Grill until light brown
  5. Flip and repeat
  6. Enjoy!
